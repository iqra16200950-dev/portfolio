About

I’m a 14-year-old student and artist who enjoys creating visual identities. I’ve been passionate about art since kindergarten, and that passion has stayed with me throughout my life.

As for my personal story-there’s a lot to unpack, so let me begin.

I’m Iqra (obviously, lol). I was born prematurely on 29th December 2011 in the beautiful lands of Saudi Arabia. Culturally, I’m Indian—my roots and ancestry are firmly tied there,but personally, my identity feels a little more complicated… maybe even a bit confusing. After my birth, I lived in Saudi Arabia for a while before moving to India for a year of kindergarten. And then suddenly—BOOM—I was in the UAE. Living in Dubai for around nine years shaped me deeply; it raised my standards, broadened my perspective, and played a huge role in who I am today. I studied in the same school for those nine years, and it truly became my second home. It’s where I grew : not just academically, but as a person.

Then came another BOOM,I had to move back to India. Even though I am Indian, it’s a bit embarrassing to admit that I was upset. Leaving meant leaving everything:my home, my school, my life there.And that wasn’t easy at all.

There were definitely ups and downs, but over time, I’ve found my footing again. And now, I’ve started to find happiness here too.

Hobbies

My hobbies are kind of a mix of creativity, chaos, and me trying to do everything at once,but like, in a fun way.

I’m really into art, especially logo design and graphic design, because I love turning random ideas into something that actually looks clean and meaningful and also slays.

At the same time, I enjoy essay writing since it lets me go on long thought tangents and put my opinions into words,and it’s something I’ve been recognized for too, with a commendation in the John Locke Essay Writing Competition (which still feels a bit unreal, honestly).

I’m also super involved in MUN-both as a delegate and a chair,which basically means I like arguing professionally, researching global issues, and pretending I have my life together in formal speeches. Debate and public speaking are a big part of that too, and even though they can be intense, I genuinely enjoy the adrenaline and the confidence boost. I’ve also managed to maintain a 100% placement record in MUNs, which is something I’m really proud of.

Outside of that, I’ve been learning Arabic for around 8 years, which is both challenging and really cool, and I actually like math (yes, voluntarily), especially the problem-solving part of it.

I also have a strong connection with reading, which has grown into something much bigger over time. Back in 5th grade, me and my friend group of 6 collaborated with my school librarian and principal to create our school reading club, the Royal Readers Squad (RRS). It started as a simple idea with only 25 students, but turned into a space where,now, around 130 students could come together, share books, and build a genuine love for reading. To take it a step further, we also launched a podcast called Literary Lounge, where we talk about stories, characters, and different perspectives, making reading feel more interactive and engaging.

Overall, my hobbies are a mix of creative expression, academic chaos, and a little bit of 'let me just try everything and see what sticks...' and somehow it all works together lol.
